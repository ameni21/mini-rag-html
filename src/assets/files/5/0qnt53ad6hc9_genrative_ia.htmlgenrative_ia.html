What is Generative AI?

Generative AI refers to artificial intelligence systems capable of creating new content, including text, images, music, and even code. These models are trained on vast datasets and use deep learning techniques, such as neural networks, to generate realistic outputs.

Applications of Generative AI

  • Text Generation (Chatbots, Story Writing)
  • Image Synthesis (AI Art, Deepfake Creation)
  • Music Composition
  • Code Generation
  • Medical Research and Drug Discovery
